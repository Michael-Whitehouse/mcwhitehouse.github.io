Summary of research

I'm a PhD student at the Centre for Doctoral Training in Computational Statistics and Data Science at the University of Bristol, under the supervision of Professor Nick Whiteley.

My research is focused on principled approximate inference in compartmental models. More broadly, I am interested in inference in models for which the true likelihood is inaccessible.
